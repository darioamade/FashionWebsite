In 1984, founders set off on an important journey called the ‘Grand Tour’. Exclusive trunk shows were staged all over the world, where the new collection was presented to politicians, businessmen and actors. The ‘Grand Tour’ attracted the company’s most important clients, including many Hollywood actors with whom the founders had established personal relationships, including Clark Gable, John Wayne and Cary Grant. The tour cemented global reputation and was met with such success that the event was repeated in the decades to come.
